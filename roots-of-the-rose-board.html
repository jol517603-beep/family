🎴 How to Play

  1. Open Bibi's envelope — 20 character cards inside.
  2. Read each story 💬 → match the S# on the tree → place card on the silhouette + write the name.
  3. The 5 round gold frames 🟡 are heirloom slots. Some heirlooms are tucked inside certain character cards. You may open up to 5 cards. Choose by deduction.
  4. The ? circle is the puzzle. Solve the lines to know whose card hides it.
