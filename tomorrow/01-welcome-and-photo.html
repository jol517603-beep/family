The Challenge

Take one group photo of all 5 team members. Use AI to transform it: every person in full baba-nyonya costume, standing inside a Peranakan mansion. The team with the most accurate portrait — correct kebaya, correct sarong, kerongsang in the right place, beaded shoes, sanggul hair, real Peranakan mansion tiles — wins the prize.

How (15 minutes)

  1. Stand together. Take one good group photo, all 5 faces visible.
  2. On any phone, open Nano Banana (Google Gemini app) — free, fast.
  3. Upload your photo. Paste the prompt below.
  4. Generate. Re-prompt if it looks wrong. You have 15 minutes.
  5. WhatsApp the final image to Bibi at the number on her table tent.
  6. Bibi will print all 5 portraits on the Canon Selphy during Game 1.

Bibi + 2 family elders will judge. Tiebreaker = clap-meter from the room. Criteria, in order:

2. Mansion background — real Peranakan elements (tiles, teak, lanterns), not random Asian decor.
3. Faces preserved — all 5 still recognisable, no swaps or distortions.
4. Composition — looks like a real heritage family photograph.
