★ GAME 1 · TEAM INSTRUCTIONS ★

Build the Family Tree

~ 50 minutes · 5 teams · 1 tree · 5 boxes ~

1 What's on your table

Your team has: 4 character cards (numbered) · 4 story cards (lettered) · 1 lockbox · markers · blue tack.

The whiteboard at the front has the title 陳家根譜 · Tan's Family Tree. It is empty. By the end of this game, all 5 teams will have built one shared tree on it.

2 Match faces to stories ~15 min

Read each of your 4 stories aloud as a team. Match each story (letter) to a character (number). Talk about it — the answers are not obvious. When you all agree, write the pair down: "A = #7", etc.

3 Build the tree together ~25 min

Stand. Bring your 4 matched characters to the whiteboard. Talk to the other 4 teams — characters from different teams are married, are parents and children, are siblings. Stick the cards on the whiteboard with blue tack and draw the lines with markers.

Generation 1 at the top (Keo San and his wives). Generation 2 below. Generation 3. Generation 4 at the bottom. Marriages: cards side by side with a horizontal line. Children: vertical line down to them.

The tree does not have to be perfect. It has to be honest.

4 Crack the code ~10 min

Every lockbox opens with the same 3-digit code. We give you the first digit. The other two are hidden in the family tree you just built.

★ THE LOCK CODE ★
3 · ? · ?
first digit given · find the other two in the tree

Digit 1 = 3   — three Yap sisters · one set of kerongsang (Ibu + 2 Anak).

Digit 2 = ?   — count the generations of the Tan family on the whiteboard. From Keo San at the top, all the way down to the youngest child. How many rows?

Digit 3 = ?   — count the petals of Mei's peony below. (The same flower is on her character card #3 — embroidered on her apron.)

Mei's peony
Count the petals around the gold centre.
(don't count the centre · only the petals)
If stuck on digit 2: ask Bibi for "the family clock" hint.
If stuck on digit 3: ask Bibi for "the flower in Mei's hand" hint.

5 Open the box together

When your team thinks you have the code, do not open yet. When all 5 teams are ready, Bibi will count down — 3, 2, 1 — and all 5 boxes open at the same time. Inside each box: one of Auntie Mei's recipes. Different for every team. Read it aloud. That is your inheritance.
