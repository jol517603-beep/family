★ BIBI'S SCRIPT · KEEP IN APRON ★

Welcome → Photo Challenge → Game 1 starts

~ 1:00 PM to 1:30 PM ~
1
Welcome the room
1:00 · 5 min

Stand in front of the whiteboard. Wait for the room to settle.

"Selamat datang. Welcome home — even if some of you have never been to this home before. Today is the Tan family reunion. Twenty-six of you, five teams, three and a half hours, and at the end, you will leave with three things: a recipe, a piece of batik, and a photograph that does not yet exist."
"Look at the envelope you took at the door. Whatever item is inside — kerongsang, tingkat, kasut manik, kamcheng, baju kebaya — that is your team's symbol. If you got Babi Pongteh, you are the wildcard. Pick any team. They cannot say no. That is also Peranakan."
2
Grouping
1:05 · 5 min
"Now find your team. A team is five people, five different items. No duplicates. Walk, talk, hold up your envelope. You have five minutes."
Do: let them mingle. Help anyone left out. Once teams of 5 are set, point them to the long table — each corner has a table tent with a team name.
3
Brief the AI Photo Challenge
1:10 · 15 min
"Before the game begins — one team challenge. Take a group photo of your five. Use AI on your phone — Nano Banana, Gemini — and dress your team in full baba-nyonya costume, inside a Peranakan mansion. The full instructions are on your team table. WhatsApp the final photo to me. The team with the most accurate portrait wins a prize at the end. You have 15 minutes. Go."
Do: walk between teams. Help anyone whose phone is slow. As photos arrive on your WhatsApp, send to Selphy and start printing. (Selphy takes ~1 min per print = 5 min total. Let it run during Game 1.)
4
Open the game
1:30 · 2 min
"Photos sent? Good. Now — to the long table. In front of you: four character cards, four stories, one lockbox, markers, blue tack. The instructions are on the sheet on your table. Read it together. The board is empty on purpose. By 2:20pm, you will have built one family tree, cracked one lock code, and opened five boxes. Inside each box is something Auntie Mei left for you. Begin."
Do: set timer for 50 minutes. Game 1 runs to 2:20. Walk between teams. Drop hints when asked. (See cheat sheet for hint lines.)

★ BIBI'S SCRIPT · GAME 1 RUN ★

Run the game · Open the boxes

~ 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM ~
5
During the game · roam & hint
1:30 – 2:15 · 45 min

Walk between teams. Don't hover. Drop hints only when a team has been stuck 5+ minutes. Whisper, don't announce.

"That brooch in story G — have you read story I yet? They are sisters."
"The watch travels through the men of the family — father, son, grandson."
"Auntie Mei never sat at the long table. Where on the tree do you think she belongs?"
Halfway clap (2:00pm): "Halfway! Twenty-five minutes left. Twenty cards on the board?"
Five-min warning (2:10pm): "Five minutes. Don't stop matching — start cracking the code now too."
6
The Lock-open Moment
2:15 · 5 min

Stop them. Walk to the centre of the table.

"Pencils down. Look at what you built — twenty faces, four generations, lines of marriage and lines of blood. You did this. Some of you got it right, some beautifully wrong. Both are fine."
"Now — the code. Each team, hold your lockbox in front of you. Do you have your three digits?"

Pause. Let them check. Walk between teams whispering — confirm if asked.

"The code is the same for every box. We open together. Three… two… one… open."
If a team fails: walk over, whisper "three, four, nine", let them open. Nobody loses this round.
7
The Recipe Reveal
2:20 · 10 min

All boxes open. Each team is holding a recipe card. Lower your voice.

"Inside each box is a different recipe. Five recipes — kuih talam, kuih kaya, kuih lapis, kuih dadar, kuih bingka ubi. Each one written by Auntie Mei. Lim Mei Lan — the woman who cooked for this family for seventy years and whose name was not in any registry until 1992. She left these for you. Read your team's recipe out loud. One person per team. Stand."
Do: let each team read. Don't rush. After all 5 are read:
"This is what you inherit tonight. Not gold. Not land. A piece of paper with her handwriting. Cook it. Teach it to one person. That is how she stays alive."
8
Prizes
2:25 · 5 min

Three prizes. (Bibi's cheat sheet has criteria.)

"Best Family Tree — to the team that placed the most cards correctly. [Award.]"
"Most Accurate AI Portrait — to the team whose AI photo looked most like a real Peranakan family heirloom. [Award.]"
"Most Heart — to the team that helped others, told the most stories, made the most noise. [Award.]"
"And now — tea. Thirty minutes. Eat the kueh. Look at the tree. Take photos. We meet back here at ten past three for batik."
