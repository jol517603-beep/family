5 Questions at the 15-Minute Mark

~ ask at 1:45 PM · 1 chocolate coin per correct answer ~
How to run this: at the 15-minute mark of Round 1, clap once. "Pencils down. Whether your tree is finished or not — five questions. First team to shout the right answer wins one gold coin." Also accept: "the Ibu" · "Tua-Ma" · "the eldest Yap" · "card #2" · "story I"
3
What was the wharf men's nickname for Auntie Mei's son — the boy born in the kitchen, never sent to school, who walked into the jungle north of Kulim after the surrender in 1945?
ANSWER Seoy Yee Kia · 細姨仔 · "the mistress's son" Tan Ah Cai · 陳亞財 · #9 (story F)
Also accept: any pronunciation of Seoy Yee Kia / Sai Yee Kia / 細姨仔 · "the mistress's son" · "Ah Cai"
4
In which year did the kerongsang — Ibu, Anak A, Anak B — finally sit complete on one collar for the first time… and on whose collar?
