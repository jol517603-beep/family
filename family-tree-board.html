📜 How to Play

  1. Open Bibi's envelope — 20 character cards inside.
  2. Read each card's story 💬 → match the same S# on the tree → place the card on that silhouette.
  3. Write the name on the dotted line. Fill the pink relationship band between each pair.
  4. Spot the 3 round gold frames 🟡 — those are heirloom slots. Hunt the room and place the matching item!
