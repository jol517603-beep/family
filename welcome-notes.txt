Today, you have inherited a Kamcheng.

A Kamcheng is a traditional Peranakan porcelain container often brought by a bride into her new home as part of her wedding dowry.

In many Peranakan families, the Kamcheng symbolized prosperity, family abundance, and the beginning of a new household.

It carried not only food and delicacies, but also traditions passed quietly from one generation to another.
