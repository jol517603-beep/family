🎯 Your 4 Missions

1
Place the 20 faces on the tree
Match each character card (red number 1–20) to its silhouette on the family tree. One slot is locked 🔒 — leave that card aside for now. write the name on the silhouette · or place the card on top
2
Find the 3rd brooch (📌 ANAK B)
The mystery brooch is engraved 1900. Find the silhouette whose birth year is 1900. The brooch was hidden in her daughter's card. circle the daughter's card # on your worksheet
3
Crack the 5 riddles in Bibi's note
Each riddle points to one card on the tree. Solve all five → you may open the locked recipe box 🔒 and reveal who Auntie Mei really is. write the card # next to each riddle on the worksheet
4
🪡 Sew the Manik → reveal a lock digit
While teammates crack the riddles, hands keep moving. Each member sews 20 lines of beads on the cloth strip — the way Nyonya girls did before their wedding slippers. Rotate every few minutes so everyone sews and everyone solves.

When all strips are laid side by side in card-number order, the bead colours form a single digit — that's the 3rd number of the padlock on Bibi's recipe box. (Hint: it's 7. Don't tell them.) pass the needle clockwise · count your 20 lines · lay strips in order · read the digit

🔒 The Sealed Recipe Box do not open

📜🔒
The locked tin on the table holds Bibi's recipe book of 1992 — and the answer to who Auntie Mei really was. Solve all 4 missions (yes, including the beads!) → Bibi will open it for you.
The padlock has 3 digits. Each mission gives you one:
➊ Mission 2 · brooch year → the 1st digit (year 1900 → 9)
➋ Mission 3 · 5 riddles → the 2nd digit (count the riddles → 5)
➌ Mission 4 · manik strips → the 3rd digit (🪡 → 7)
🔓 Combination: 9 – 5 – 7

Inside the box: Auntie Mei's real name · her bond to Lim Soo Hui · the empty-plate story · a taste of the family's secret kueh.
