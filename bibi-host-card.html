The Whiteboard Setup

~ no board to print · just chalk-pen and a flat surface ~

Before they arrive · 5 minutes

~ on a whiteboard, mahjong paper, kraft roll, or even a clean wall with painter's tape ~
  1. Find a flat surface at least 1m × 1.5m. Whiteboard, flip-chart, or a sheet of mahjong paper taped to the wall.
  2. Draw 4 horizontal bands across it (see sketch below). Label them Generation 1, 2, 3, 4 down the left side.
  3. Write the year range under each label — 1880–1930, 1900–1960, 1940–1990, 1980–today.
  4. In one corner, write the Line Legend (5 lines — see right side below).
  5. Place a roll of masking tape + 5 coloured markers (rose, jade, tan, gold, pencil) on the table.

What it should look like

~ rough sketch of your whiteboard ~
Gen 1the founders ~1880–1930 · 3 names
Gen 2their children ~1900–1960 · 6 names
Gen 3parents of today ~1940–1990 · 7 names
Gen 4tonight's reunion ~1980–today · 4 names

Line legend

~ write this in one corner ~
  • marriage · solid rose
  • parent → child · solid jade
  • siblings · dashed tan
  • adopted / kept · dotted gold
  • secret line · pencil only

What to put out

~ everything else stays in the kitchen ~
  • Identity cards · 1 set (3 A4 sheets, pre-cut into 20)
  • Story cards A–T · 1 set (4 A4 sheets, pre-cut)
  • Bibi's answer key · stays in your apron pocket
  • Masking tape · for sticking cards to the board
  • 5 markers · rose, jade, tan, gold + 1 pencil
  • This card · stays face-down on the side table

How play goes · ~30 minutes

  1. Team picks a story card, reads it aloud, finds the matching identity card.
  2. Tape the identity card on the right generation band.
  3. Draw the relationship line to whoever it connects to, using the legend.
  4. When all 20 are up and the lines are drawn — call Bibi.
