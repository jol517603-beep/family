✦ how to play ✦

Build the Tree, Find the Truth

~ 15 minutes · then Bibi walks ~
1
Read
Pick a story card (A–T). Read it aloud to the team.
2
Match
Find the matching identity card (#1–#20). Discuss before you decide.
3
Pin
Pin the story card beside the identity card on the board, in the right generation.
4
Wait
When all 20 are up, raise a hand. Bibi will come and ask. Right answer = gold coin.
Generations  ·  1 founders ~1880–1930  ·  2 children ~1900–1960  ·  3 parents ~1940–1990  ·  4 tonight ~1980–today
