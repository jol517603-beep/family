🌺 The Yap Sisters' Secret

The youngest sister, Yap Eng Hooi 葉永慧, did not die in winter 1923. She fell in love with a Singapore man the family would not accept and left the goldsmith house.

The "death" was the eldest sister's cover. The grave at Batu Gantong holds only her brooch box.

She had one daughter — born 1924, raised under another surname. That daughter married a Khoo. Her granddaughter is #19 Khoo Hooi Wen.

When Hooi Wen wore the 1948 slippers at her 2020 wedding, she closed two broken marriages — Aunt Chen Neo's eleven months in Singapore, and her own great-grandmother's elopement.

"Yap–Khoo, Khoo–Yap. The names always come back together."
